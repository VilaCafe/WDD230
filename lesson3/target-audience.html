Target Audience

About the target audience

Residents of Lages/SC and residents of the entire mountain region of Santa Catarina, as well as visitors who want to learn more about the city or look for tourist spots, events and so on.

Two Personas

A 30-year-old man who writes for a travel blog. He often writes about tourist cities, the best routes and best places, such as restaurants, hotels and other points of the city.

A university student who is about to complete her degree and is looking for job openings in her area of expertise.

Five Scenarios

  • A tourist comes to the city and wants information about hotels and inns where he can stay during a typical party in the region.
  • A group of young people are looking for the best route to hike in the mountains of the city.
  • Residents look for job opportunities in the city.
  • A resident of a neighboring city is looking for a hospital or doctor, for a specific health area, which is not available in his own city.
  • A local resident wants to know about promotions in the city's commerce.
